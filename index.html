01 — Work Hard

Alex Satchwell

Operations  ·  Support Office

How winners are chosen

The Leadership Team reviews the shortlist each quarter and selects one winner per value. Winners receive a £75 Oliver Bonas voucher and are celebrated in the Team Update.

The bar

We're looking for team members who've done something genuinely beyond their role — not just people doing their jobs well. If it made you think "wow, they didn't have to do that" — that's the one.
